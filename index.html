    The DogMother

    Valentina Talor

    Classically trained since age 5, Valentina learned early that music says what a thousand words can’t. When the world tried to shut her up, control her, contain her — she sang louder.

    Her songs are about survival, overcoming, and breaking the chains of narcissists and abusers. A mom. A wife. An empty nester. A Gen X’er who’s been through it all and turned every scar into a song.

    Complicated. Scarred. Still standing.

    JT

    Featured Artist

    Tatted up, rough around the edges, and zero apologies. JT is a former bull rider who hung up his spurs — but that cowboy grit never left. Scars on his heart, fire in his throat, and a voice that sounds like whiskey poured over gravel.

    His songs cut deep — bad exes, parental alienation, cowboy blues, and telling people exactly where to shove it. Not politically correct. Full of sarcasm, full of truth, and always up for a good fight.
