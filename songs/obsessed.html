The Story Behind This Song

Years later and they're still scrolling your page at 2 AM. Still stalking. Still smearing your name to anyone who'll listen. Still gossiping like it's their full-time job. This song is about that ex who never moved on — the one who can't stop watching your life even though they lost the right to be in it. You left. You healed. You grew. And they're still obsessed.
