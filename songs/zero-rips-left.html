The Story Behind This Song

Same energy as zero fucks but with more surf. When the ocean takes the last of your pain and you float back with nothing left to tear. I wrote this after a day in the water where I screamed into the waves until my voice was gone — and when I came out, I was lighter than I'd been in years.
