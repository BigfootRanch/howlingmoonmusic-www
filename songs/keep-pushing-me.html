The Story Behind This Song

Door slam shakes these walls tonight. This song is for every woman who stayed too long, took too much, and finally snapped. It's not about violence — it's about the fire that burns inside when someone pushes you past your limit. TikTok and YouTube keep banning the video. They call it violent. We call it survival. Watch the uncensored version only at howlingmoonmusic.com.
