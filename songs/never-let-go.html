The Story Behind This Song

They talked about us in corners. Judged us. Tried to tell us what we were wasn't real. But God saw what He joined that night — two shadows and a flame — and heaven smiled while the world looked away. This is the outlaw love song. The one where you grab someone's hand and say 'I'll never let go' and mean it so deep it scares you. No walls, no rings, no earthly worth. Just two souls colliding to the end of the earth.
