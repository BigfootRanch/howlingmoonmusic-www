The Story Behind This Song

The uncensored version lives here and only here. 'Enjoy that shit pie, baby, take a bite — dessert's on me, and it just ain't right.' This song is for every woman who smiled through the abuse, swallowed the lies, and finally decided to serve them back. Door slam shakes these walls tonight — but this time, I'm the one walking out.
