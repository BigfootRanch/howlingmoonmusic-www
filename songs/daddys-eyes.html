The Story Behind This Song

JT wrote this one — and he could barely get through recording it.

This song is about a father who had his child ripped away from him through parental alienation. Not because he was dangerous. Not because he didn't love her. Because someone decided to weaponize a child against the man who used to mow the yard with her strapped to his back like a butterfly.

She filed the papers. Blocked the calls. Burned the photos. Told the child daddy was gone. But blood doesn't lie. And somewhere out there, that little girl still has her daddy's eyes — the one thing they couldn't erase.

If you've ever had your child taken from you by the system, by lies, by someone who turned love into a weapon — this song is yours. You're not forgotten. And neither is your child.
