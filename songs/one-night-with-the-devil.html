The Story Behind This Song

This is part two of I Didn't Die.

They branded her scarlet. Burned her alive with lies. Every person she thought loved her stood in the circle and watched. The church. The family. The town. They all turned their backs.

And then the devil showed up.

He wasn't her plan. He wasn't her type. He was the one they warned her about — the blamed-up sinner with a steady hand. But while everyone else was casting stones, he was catching her tears before they hit her lips.

Small-town gossip lit the spark. But the forbidden love story that came out of the ashes? That's the fire that saved her life. The devil ended up being her saving grace. And heaven's been jealous ever since.

They said we'd burn. We made love grow. One night with the devil, one night divine.
