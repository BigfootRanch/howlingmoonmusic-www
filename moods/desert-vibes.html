Desert Sunset Vibes

There's a frequency in the desert that you can't find anywhere else. The Organ Mountains at sunset. Canyon Road at dusk. The green of the juniper against red earth after rain. These songs were written on dusty highways and in small-town cantinas across New Mexico -- where the sky is bigger, the stars are closer, and the music sounds like the land it came from.
